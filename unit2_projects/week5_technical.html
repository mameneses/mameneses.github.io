Ruby Staying Classy

4/5/2014

Today we are going to focus on Ruby classes, what they are and how they can be used. We are doing this today because ruby classes are awesome and so are you.

The class in ruby can essentially be seen as your subject or a layout. The Class is the thing that encompasses what all of the code within the class is about. Within your class you define what characteristics, also known as variables, make up the class and what functions your class. Your class can model real life subjects (vehicles, sports teams, desserts) or anything that you can think up.
