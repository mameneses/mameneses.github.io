Wireframe Sites

Mint.com

This specific page is solving the problem of welcoming people to the site and trying to get them to sign up. It has the visual challenge of keeping a user engaged and leading them to the right information about signing and addressing any doubts that may arise.

Footytube.com

This page, the home page of footytube, solves the problem providing the most current information and news about the soccer world while also guiding them to the most popular content, the videos. It has the visual challenge of not overwhelming the reader with information while still appealing to different audiences.

Pitchfork.com

This page, also the home page, solves the problem of introducing the user to its content, showing current and pertinent information and easily guiding people to new music. It faces the visual challenge of providing enough information and visuals to keep the user interested while not bombarding the user.
